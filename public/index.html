About the Email Marketing Service:

Is email marketing worth it?

Ad costs are rising for everyone. With CPA getting higher and higher there’s only so much you can squeeze out of your ad manager. So the easiest way to afford to run more ads is to increase your customer lifetime value (LTV). Email marketing just happens to be the easiest way to increase your customer LTV. Bigger LTV = higher margins = bigger ad budget = bigger business.

Who do you typically partner with?

I typically partner with businesses who have an email list, are running ads and generate at least $20k/month in revenue.

Are you an agency?

No, the Email Operator is run and operated by Augustinas Venclovas. While the agency model can work, I believe it is flawed and doesn’t serve the best interests of the brand or the professionals who provide the service.

No. We will agree on a monthly retainer and should we part ways, we will give the other party a two-week courtesy notice. We part as friends and you also get to keep everything I built for your brand.

Will you have time to work on my business?

Yes. To ensure I have the time, energy and creative capacity to give your business the attention it deserves, I intentionally limit the number of businesses I partner with to three (3) at a time.

Yes. Once your email marketing is in a good place, we can look into introducing SMS marketing.
