Meet the Email Operator

Augustinas Venclovas

Augustinas is an expert email marketer with experience generating millions in email revenue for his clients. After working with some of the best email marketing agencies in the world… and witnessing their strengths and shortcomings… Augustinas has ditched the agency model and started the Email Operator - a white-glove done-for-you email marketing service.

Augustinas combines his skills as a former direct-response copywriter and front-end engineer to create and implement sophisticated email marketing strategies that increase email revenue immediately and long-term.

The Email Operator Focuses on 3 Core Fundamentals to Grow Your Revenue

Email Deliverability

Some emails end up in promotions. Others get marked as spam. Your emails will confidently land in your subscribers’ primary inbox for maximum open rates and sales.

Better Automations

Good email flows can keep a business afloat. Great email flows will generate enough revenue for you to scale your ad spend and grow your business. It’s time to make your email automations reach their full potential.

Nurturing Campaigns

Save the big discounts and flash sales for special occasions. Sending meaningful, on-brand emails will drive consistent sales while allowing you to keep a bigger profit margin.
